    Creating Banners

    We have different styles of banners so you can pick the one that matches your audience best. Let's post a banner on your site

    1. Login

    Login to your account at Lazada.hasoffers.com. Click on Offers then Browse/Search.

    2. See Philippine Offers

    Click on Browse by Category, then Philippine Offers. Press Apply.

    3. See All Banners

    Latest banners (we call them campaigns) are on top. When you're done click the campaign you chose.

    4. Get your banner code

    Scroll down to Creatives and select the banner size you want. Click Get Code on the right.

    5. Post!

    Paste your banner code/link online on your website! Here's an example of how you can use your banner & text links well!
